In this work i call it the size because the circle keep change like big to small to big. And the odd number of the circle is slightly different from the even number of the circle. Also, the color I make it is also different, like the big one get orangle and red, and the small one will get an orange and yellow gradient. In animation, that i have used translate to move the location of the shape and the scale to change the size of the shape. This idea of a circle comes from the assignment when we need to write about one artist we find interesting, which I have picked Rafaël Rozendaal's artwork, which gives me an idea to do a circle changing color. The challenge I have faced is with the direction when trying to move the circle in the direction that I want. But in the end i end up with a zip zap motion of the circle.
